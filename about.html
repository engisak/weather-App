About

Weather Dashboard waa web app fudud oo ku siinaya xogta cimilada maanta iyo 5 maalmood ee soo socda, adigoo isticmaalaya API-ga OpenWeatherMap. Mashruucan waxaa loo sameeyay in lagu barto JavaScript, APIs, iyo sida loogu kaydiyo search history-ga localStorage. Ujeedada mashruuca waa in uu noqdo tusaale wax ku ool ah oo ku saabsan sida loo sameeyo project dhameystiran oo ku saleysan HTML, CSS, iyo JavaScript.
